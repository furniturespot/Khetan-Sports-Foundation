About Khetan Sports Foundation


Khetan Sports Foundation (KSF) is a non-profit organization committed for transforming grassroots sports in Nepal. By supporting young athletes, especially from underrepresented communities, KSF promotes equal opportunity and inclusive growth. Through partnerships, training, and expert-led programs, we enable athletes to thrive on and off the field. Our mission is to build a strong sporting ecosystem driven by passion, discipline, and innovation.

Areas of focus

We have directed our initiatives towards creating an impact in the grassroots sports ecosystem.

Grassroots sports support

We work with our partner academies, focusing on grassroots sports. We provide the necessary interventions to help athletes with the all-around development they need to fulfil their sporting dreams.

Sports science interventions

We provide our partner academies access to sports science experts. Strength & conditioning coaches, nutritionists and sports psychologists interact with athletes through online and offline workshops.

Women in Sports

We promote the participation of women in sports in India by addressing the underlying issues like access and stereotypes through different channels.

SAI partnership

We have partnered with Sports Authority of India to provide support in the areas of sports science, equipment, expertise, tech support, and research to amplify our impact and reach.

About Us – Khetan Sports Foundation


Khetan Sports Foundation is a passionate not-for-profit organization dedicated to transforming lives through the power of sports. Founded with a vision to build an inclusive, vibrant sporting culture in India, the foundation is focused on promoting grassroots development, empowering young athletes, and addressing the gaps in access, opportunity, and awareness within the Indian sports ecosystem.


At the heart of our mission lies the belief that sports can be a powerful agent of change — encouraging discipline, teamwork, leadership, and resilience. We aim to harness these qualities to help shape confident individuals who not only strive for sporting excellence but also grow into responsible, empowered citizens. Through carefully designed programs, outreach initiatives, and partnerships with schools, academies, coaches, and communities, Khetan Sports Foundation supports the holistic development of youth across the country.


Our initiatives span across multiple dimensions. We offer professional training and coaching support, enabling young talent — particularly those from underrepresented or economically challenged backgrounds — to access opportunities that were previously out of reach. We believe that talent exists everywhere, and with the right guidance and infrastructure, anyone can rise and shine on national and international platforms.


Beyond just training, we focus on athlete well-being through nutrition awareness, mental health support, and sports science interventions. We work closely with experts such as dieticians, physiotherapists, and psychologists to ensure that each athlete receives a well-rounded developmental experience. Our programs are tailored to build sustainable ecosystems that not only nurture future champions but also strengthen community engagement with sports at large.


In our efforts to foster inclusion, we pay special attention to the challenges faced by female athletes. Through education, mentorship, and targeted campaigns, we work to eliminate stigma around issues like menstruation and promote gender equality in sports. Our “Women in Sports” initiative actively engages girls and women from various communities, offering them the confidence and support to pursue sports fearlessly.


Collaboration is key to our success. We have partnered with national and regional sports organizations, educational institutions, and thought leaders to amplify our impact. Through joint ventures, workshops, training camps, and tournaments, we continue to reach more aspiring athletes and build a network of support that is both scalable and sustainable.


Khetan Sports Foundation is more than just a sports NGO — it’s a movement to inspire a generation. Our work is driven by a passionate team of coaches, volunteers, strategists, and community leaders who believe in the transformative magic of sports. With each child we support, each barrier we break, and each community we uplift, we take a step closer to our vision of a fitter, stronger, and more united India.


We invite you to be a part of this journey. Whether you are a young athlete, a mentor, a partner, or a supporter — your involvement can spark change. Together, let’s champion a future where every child has the opportunity to dream, play, and excel.
