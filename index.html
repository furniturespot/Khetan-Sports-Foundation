About Khetan Sports Foundation


Khetan Sports Foundation (KSF) is a non-profit organization committed for transforming grassroots sports in Nepal. By supporting young athletes, especially from underrepresented communities, KSF promotes equal opportunity and inclusive growth. Through partnerships, training, and expert-led programs, we enable athletes to thrive on and off the field. Our mission is to build a strong sporting ecosystem driven by passion, discipline, and innovation.

Areas of focus

We have directed our initiatives towards creating an impact in the grassroots sports ecosystem.

Grassroots sports support

We work with our partner academies, focusing on grassroots sports. We provide the necessary interventions to help athletes with the all-around development they need to fulfil their sporting dreams.

Sports science interventions

We provide our partner academies access to sports science experts. Strength & conditioning coaches, nutritionists and sports psychologists interact with athletes through online and offline workshops.

Women in Sports

We promote the participation of women in sports in India by addressing the underlying issues like access and stereotypes through different channels.

SAI partnership

We have partnered with Sports Authority of India to provide support in the areas of sports science, equipment, expertise, tech support, and research to amplify our impact and reach.

About Us – Khetan Sports Foundation


Khetan Sports Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to transforming lives through sports in Nepal. With a focus on grassroots development, it empowers young athletes and promotes an inclusive, vibrant sporting culture by bridging gaps in access, opportunity, and awareness.


KSF believes in the power of sports to drive positive change - instilling discipline, teamwork, leadership, & resilience. Our mission is to develop confident, responsible youth through tailored programs, community outreach, & partnerships with schools, academies & coaches across country.


We empower young talent from underrepresented and economically challenged backgrounds with professional training, coaching, and holistic support, including nutrition, mental health, and sports science. We aim to develop future champions and build a thriving, sport-focused community.


KSF also promotes gender equality in sports by addressing challenges faced by female athletes. Through education, mentorship, and targeted campaigns, we eliminate stigma around issues like menstruation and empower women to pursue sports confidently.


Our “Women in Sports” initiative supports girls and women across communities, fostering inclusivity and strength. We are deeply committed to supporting the cause by creating a platform that empowers women and girls to break barriers and achieve their fullest potential in the sporting world. Here’s how the foundation plays a crucial role in supporting this cause.


Collaboration drives our success. By partnering with national and regional sports organizations, schools, and thought leaders, we amplify our impact. Through joint ventures, workshops, training camps, and tournaments, we support aspiring athletes and build a scalable, sustainable network. We are in a movement inspiring a generation through sports. Powered by dedicated coaches, volunteers, and community leaders, we break barriers, uplift communities, and support every child, all towards building a fitter, stronger, and united Nepal.


Join us on this journey! Whether you're an athlete, mentor, partner, or supporter, your involvement can drive change. Together, let's create a future where every child can dream, play, and excel.


OUR MISSION


The mission of Khetan Sports Foundation is to create sustainable sports systems in Nepal through impactful campaigns, seminars, and events. the foundation aims to empower youth, promote sports awareness, and build a strong, inclusive sports culture from the ground up. Our mission is to bridge the opportunity gap in sports by offering professional guidance and infrastructure to youth, regardless of their background. KSF's team blends dynamic young professionals with experienced consultants from sports federations, former players, media, and business sectors. This unique mix provides fresh and effective solutions to grassroots sports challenges in Nepal.


OUR VISION


"Our vision is to create an inclusive and thriving sports ecosystem where every young athlete, regardless of background, has the opportunity to reach their full potential. We aim to break barriers, foster gender equality, and empower the next generation of champions who will shine on national and international stages. This vision seeks to foster a culture where every child has access to quality sports training, nurturing physical, mental, and social growth, and enabling them to pursue their athletic potential to the fullest. By making sports an integral part of education, the foundation aims to shape a healthier, more active, and competitive generation for Nepal’s future.
