About Khetan Sports Foundation


Khetan Sports Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to transforming lives through sports in Nepal. With a focus on grassroots development, it empowers young athletes and promotes an inclusive, vibrant sporting culture by bridging gaps in access, opportunity, and awareness. KSF believes in the power of sports to drive positive change - instilling discipline, teamwork, leadership, & resilience. Our mission is to develop confident, responsible youth through tailored programs, community outreach, & partnerships with schools, academies & coaches across country.


We empower young talent from underrepresented and economically challenged backgrounds with professional training, coaching, and holistic support, including nutrition, mental health, and sports science. We aim to develop future champions and build a thriving, sport-focused community.


KSF also promotes gender equality in sports by addressing challenges faced by female athletes. Through education, mentorship, and targeted campaigns, we eliminate stigma around issues like menstruation and empower women to pursue sports confidently. Our “Women in Sports” initiative supports girls and women across communities, fostering inclusivity and strength. We are deeply committed to supporting the cause by creating a platform that empowers women and girls to break barriers and achieve their fullest potential in the sporting world. Here’s how the foundation plays a crucial role in supporting this cause.


Collaboration drives our success. By partnering with national and regional sports organizations, schools, and thought leaders, we amplify our impact. Through joint ventures, workshops, training camps, and tournaments, we support aspiring athletes and build a scalable, sustainable network. We are in a movement inspiring a generation through sports. Powered by dedicated coaches, volunteers, and community leaders, we break barriers, uplift communities, and support every child, all towards building a fitter, stronger, and united Nepal.


Join us on this journey! Whether you're an athlete, mentor, partner, or supporter, your involvement can drive change. Together, let's create a future where every child can dream, play, and excel.
